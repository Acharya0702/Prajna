Frequently Asked Questions

Here we are presenting some frequently asked questions by our users and visitors.

Our online classes are designed to be accessible to learners of various age groups. While there is no strict minimum age requirement, we generally recommend that participants be at least 10 years old to effectively follow the course material.
There is no fixed limit on the number of classes you can take at PRAJNA informatics. You are free to enroll in multiple courses or sessions based on your learning goals, availability, and interest.
Enrolling in our classes is simple and convenient. You can register by visiting our website and completing the online registration form. Once the form is submitted, our team will review your request and provide further instructions.
If you decide to discontinue your class after enrolling, you can request to drop the course by contacting our support team. Any applicable policies regarding cancellations or refunds will be communicated based on the terms of the selected program.
You will need a reliable internet connection, a computer/laptop/tablet with modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge), and a working microphone and speakers for live sessions.
The cost depends on the course type, duration and level of difficulty. Please refer to the specific course page or contact our support team for detailed fee information.
Payment is typically required at the time of enrollment to confirm your seat. For certain programs, installment options may be available.
We support debit cards, credit cards, net banking, and other digital payment methods available on our platform.
