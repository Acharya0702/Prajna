Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our plans and pricing.

Our online classes are designed to be accessible to learners of various age groups. While there is no strict minimum age requirement, we generally recommend that participants be at least 10 years old to effectively follow the course material.
There is no fixed limit on the number of classes you can take. You are free to enroll in multiple courses based on your learning goals and availability.
You can register by visiting our website and completing the online registration form. Once submitted, our team will review and provide further instructions.
You can request to drop the course by contacting our support team. Refund policies depend on the selected plan and program terms.
You need a reliable internet connection, a modern browser (Chrome/Firefox/Edge), and a working microphone/speakers for live sessions.
Pricing varies based on the plan you choose. Basic starts at $5/month, Team at $50/month, and Enterprise at $150/month with unlimited access.
We support debit/credit cards, net banking, UPI, and other digital payment methods available on our secure platform.
